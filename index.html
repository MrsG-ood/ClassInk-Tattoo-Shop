About

We are yout local tattoo shoppe that offers a comprehensive tattoo experiance. Our facilitys are clean adhearing to the most strict standards. The artists have a combined 30+ years of experaince creating tattoo art. Our artists use state of the art tools, inks and materials to create a one of a kind piece just for you.

Process

The very first thing to do is to contact us to schedual a design assessment with your artist. All appoinments for tattoos will start with this process.The design session is non-refundable. Please contact us for prices. The first meeting can be in person or via video conference. This is a preliminary meeting where you will discuss with your chosen artist your tattoo experiance. Make sure you have all information handy to include:

  • design ideas
  • Colors
  • aproximate size of the tattoo
  • previous tattoos near the area for your new tattoo
  • Potential Allergic Reactions
  • Where you want the ink on your body
  • Any questions, comments or concerns
  • Payment discussion

Next: You will then recieve a mock up design of the tattoo. Once you recieve the image (via the methods agreed upon between you and your artist); you will have 48 hours to approve the design. Once you approve the design an appointmnet will be made with your artist to begin the outline and foundational application of your tattoo. Some tattoos do not take very much time an can be done in one session. Other tattoos may take several sessions. All tattoo payments must be made prior to the start of your work. We do offer payment plans. However, your payment must be fulkly made prior to the start of your tattoo being inked. Please NOTE: Some of our artists work on an hourly scale, and some price out projects based on size, colors, details and placement of the tattoo. Each artist prices thier work differently and are indepenedant of the shoppe. We proudly represent our independant artists that rent space at our location.

Notice: ClassInk Tattoo Shoppe Artists are prohibited to ink or design anti-american, or any other imagery that may be seen as offensive to the artist. We support the rights of our artists and their choice to refuse any design that goes against America, or their personal/religious/faith based morals.We will gladly refund any design assessment fees associated with designs that have been rejected. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone. However, we will not refuse service based on predjudices of race, nationality, creed, class, political views, education, or any other reason other than offensive designs or vulgar treatment of staff, artists or other customers. ClassInk Tattoo Shoppe believes that everyone has value, no matter who or what they are!
